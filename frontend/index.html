Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

We currently deliver within Lagos and its environs. Delivery is available to areas including Victoria Island, Lekki, Ikoyi, Ikeja, Surulere, Yaba, and more. Contact us to confirm if we deliver to your specific location.

Delivery typically takes 30-60 minutes within Lagos, depending on your location and traffic conditions. For bulk orders, we recommend placing your order at least 24 hours in advance.

Yes! All our products are made fresh daily using natural ingredients. We don't use artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors. Our smoothies, zobo, and yogurt parfaits are prepared on the same day to ensure maximum freshness and nutritional value.

Absolutely! We offer catering services for events of all sizes including birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and parties. We have special bulk pricing and can customize orders to suit your needs. Contact us at least 48 hours before your event.

We accept bank transfers, card payments, and cash on delivery. For online orders, you can pay securely through our website. We also accept payments via mobile money and POS on delivery.

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs. We offer dairy-free, sugar-free, and vegan options. Please mention any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order, and we'll do our best to accommodate you. Common allergens in our products may include nuts, dairy, and gluten.

Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status through your account on our website. You'll also receive SMS/WhatsApp updates as your order progresses from preparation to delivery. Our order statuses include: Pending, Confirmed, Preparing, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns. However, if there's an issue with your order (wrong item, quality concerns), please contact us immediately with photos, and we'll resolve it promptly. Customer satisfaction is our priority!
